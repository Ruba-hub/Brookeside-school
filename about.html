About Brookside School

Shaping the next generation through quality education, character, and innovation.

Our Story

BROOKSIDE school formerly E-com School was founded on 29th of September 2009 by Entebbe Pentecostal Church (EPC) a member of Pentecostal Assemblies of God (PAG) Uganda with a view of evangelizing the stake holders, achieving academic excellence, helping some pupils and to be strictly run on Christian principals. The school name and vision changed in the year 2021. The school has both nursery and primmary section running from KG1 to Primary 3.

Our commitment goes beyond the classroom — we create an environment where students thrive, explore their talents, and prepare for a successful future.

Our Core Values

What defines who we are

Spirituality

We nurture a strong spiritual foundation that guides learners toward moral discipline, purpose, and faith-driven character.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in academics, behavior, and personal growth, inspiring students to always give their best.

Time Management

We teach learners to use their time wisely, stay organized, meet deadlines, and develop habits that support long-term success.

Creativity

We encourage imagination, innovation, and original thinking that helps learners explore new ideas and solutions.

Compassion

We foster kindness, empathy, and respect—creating a caring environment where every learner feels valued.

Wisdom

We equip learners with the ability to make sound decisions, think critically, and apply knowledge responsibly in real-life situations.

Who We Are

We believe that every child is born creative. At Brookside, we nurture and further enhance this creativity—opening doors to new possibilities and empowering learners to express themselves confidently. We are genuinely concerned about one another’s needs. Our school community thrives on care, empathy, and understanding, ensuring no learner is left behind. As we seek wisdom, we compassionately create opportunities for learners to build strong character, moral discipline, and a deep sense of purpose. We guide our pupils to apply the knowledge they acquire to real-world situations—helping them become thoughtful, capable, and responsible individuals. We partner with families to support the education of their children and raise global citizens who can positively influence their communities and the world.
